A dance company.

Making the job of companies and event organizers looking for dancers easier. Building a community for the next generation of dancers.

Putting south Swedish dance on the map.

Blac Opal started out of dissatisfaction with the regional hierarchies of Sweden's commercial dance field, and a striving to make a dance career viable for dancers living far away from Stockholm and Göteborg. With a network of over 30 dancers we are, and have been, showing Sweden what the south is capable of.

Bringing dance to the people.

Blac Opal Talent Agency is the part of the company that collaborates with event and commercial agencies to provide dancers and choreographers for any and all occasions. With a small team of employees and a team of over 30 dancers and choreographers from south of Sweden, we have worked on 44 small and large scale projects for brands including IKEA, Porsche, SIME, and Casino Cosmopol. This part of the company has been put on hold for a few years now as our team has moved on to other projects, however our network of contacts still remain. Do you have an idea that you want to realize with the help of dance and choreography? Don't hesitate to get in touch!

Community building.

The other part of our company, started in 2016 with Blac Opal Summer Camp, focuses on creating a safe space for young dancers to sweat, develop, and share their love for dance. What started as a dance camp, organized during summers and winters, quickly turned into a full-fledged mentorship program, where students got to practice all-year-round and receive constant feedback from their mentor, chosen from the pool of dancers working in our agency. This part of the company is still running, although the pandemic has forced us to take a break from it. Since our beginnings, we have organized several camps and workshops with over 4000 participants in total, as well as produced a full-length dance show in collaboration with some of Skånes biggest dance organizations.
