A dance company.

Providing dance for companies and event organizers. Building a community for the next generation of dancers.

Showing people what the south is capable of.

Blac Opal started out of dissatisfaction with the regional hierarchies of Sweden's commercial dance field, and a striving to make a dance career viable for dancers living far away from big cities like Stockholm and Gothenburg. With a network of over 30 dancers we are showing Sweden, and have been for 7 years, what the south is capable of.

Providing organizers with high quality dance.

Blac Opal Talent Agency is the part of the company that collaborates with event and commercial agencies to provide dancers and choreographers for any and all productions and occasions. With a small team of employees and a team of over 30 dancers and choreographers from south of Sweden, we have worked on 44 small and large scale projects for a multitude of brands including IKEA, Porsche, SIME, and Casino Cosmopol. This part of the company has been put on hold for a few years now as our team has moved on to other projects, however our network of contacts still remain. Do you have an idea that you want to realize with the help of dance and choreography? Don't hesitate to get in touch!

Determined to grow the south swedish dance community.

The other part of our company, started in 2016 with Blac Opal Summer Camp, focuses on creating a safe space for young dancers to sweat, develop, and share their love for dance. What started as a dance camp, organized during summers and winters, quickly turned into a full-fledged mentorship program, where students got to practice all-year-round and receive constant feedback from their mentor, chosen from the pool of dancers working in our agency. This part of the company is still running, although the pandemic has forced us to take a break from it. Since our beginnings, we have organized several camps and workshops with over 4000 participants in total, as well as produced a full-length dance show in collaboration with some of Skånes biggest dance organizations.

Timeline

2015

Blac Opal Talent Agency started out with a team of approximately 10 dancers, doing small-scale performances for local clients.

2016

During 2016 we had grown the business to bigger clients and now had a team of roughly the size we have today. We also organized our first dance camp, which sold out in a few weeks and grew our reputation as dance event organizers considerably.

2017

We started our mentorship program, with weekly classes, organized more workshops and camps as well as produced a sold-out full-length show. By this point, we were a household name among young dancers in Skåne, and through Blac Opal Talent Agency we had now worked with clients like IKEA, Porsche, and SIME.

2018

2018 was one of our most active years. With more clients and events than ever, as well as with a perfected camp-formula, we now celebrated one of our best years in business.

2019

From 2019, many members of our team moved internationally, and on to other projects. We still organize dance camps once every year, but with a lower frequency than before. We still accept project inquiries and stay true to our mission though, so feel free to get in touch if you want to work with us or just have a chat.
